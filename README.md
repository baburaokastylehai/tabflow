# TabFlow

smart tab organizer for Chrome. groups your tabs by category, lets you search across everything, and focus mode cuts the noise when you need to concentrate.

![Chrome Extension](https://img.shields.io/badge/Chrome-Extension-C17C4E?style=flat&logo=googlechrome&logoColor=white)

## what it does

- **auto-categorizes tabs** into Work, Development, Social, Entertainment, and Research based on the domain
- **search** across all open tabs by title or URL
- **focus mode** — suspends your least-used tabs so you can concentrate on what matters. your tabs aren't closed, just sleeping. disable focus mode and they wake back up.
- **save & restore sessions** — snapshot all your current tabs and bring them back later
- **tab count badge** — shows how many tabs you have open right on the extension icon

## install

1. download or clone this repo
2. open Chrome and go to `chrome://extensions`
3. enable **Developer Mode** (top right toggle)
4. click **Load Unpacked**
5. select the folder you downloaded
6. pin TabFlow to your toolbar — done

## why

anyone with 20+ tabs open knows the feeling. you keep them open "just in case" and your browser turns into a graveyard of things you meant to read. TabFlow groups them so you can see what's what, and focus mode lets you clear the clutter without losing anything.

## built with

- Chrome Extension Manifest V3
- vanilla JS — no frameworks, no dependencies, no build step
- works entirely offline, no data leaves your browser
